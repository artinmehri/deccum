# Deccum Pitches

## 30-second pitch
Retirees with money in a 401(k), Roth, brokerage, pension, and Social Security still guess which account to spend first. The wrong order triggers penalties, taxes, and lost healthcare subsidies. Deccum is a withdrawal sequencing engine that tells you exactly which account to tap every year — and proves that a better order can save six figures before Medicare.

## 2-minute pitch
When someone leaves a corporate job in their 50s, their savings are rarely in one place. They’re in a 401(k), maybe an old IRA, a Roth, a taxable brokerage, some cash, a pension, and Social Security that hasn’t started yet.

Every calculator shows balances. Almost none answer the only question that matters: **which account should I withdraw from this year?**

Hiring a fee-only planner for a custom withdrawal strategy can cost thousands. Everyone else guesses.

Deccum builds a year-by-year withdrawal plan. It considers early-withdrawal penalties, the Rule of 55, Roth vs traditional treatment, brokerage gains, pension and Social Security timing, and ACA subsidy thresholds.

Then it does the killer thing: it compares a **naive** order against an **optimized** order on the same profile. In our demo, simply changing the sequence avoids tens of thousands in penalties, preserves years of ACA eligibility, and leaves six figures more at age 65.

We’re not another retirement calculator. We’re a sequencing engine. The product principle is simple: **the order you withdraw money matters.**
